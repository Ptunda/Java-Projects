Arsenal Football Club is a professional football club based in Islington, London, England. 
It was founded in 1886 and has a rich history in English football. 
Arsenal is one of the most successful clubs in English football history, having won numerous domestic league titles, including the English Premier League, FA Cups, and League Cups. 
The club has also had success in European competitions, although they haven't won the UEFA Champions League. 
Arsenal's home stadium is the Emirates Stadium, which has a capacity of over 60,000 spectators. 
The club's colors are red and white, and they are known by their nickname, "The Gunners."
