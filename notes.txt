
Three Java files:

	1. FullNamePraser: prompts user to provide their full name, extracts the user's first, middle initial, and last name from the user's full name, and then prints the information extratced.
	2. TheaterReservations: prompts user to provide their full name, when they'll be coming for the show?, how many tickest they'd like to book, and then prints a confirmation message to the user.
	3. AddressBuilder: prompts user for their full name, billing address, and shipping address; then displays a readable message to confirm the information provided.
